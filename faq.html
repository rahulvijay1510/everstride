Questions, answered

Frequently asked questions

Honest answers to what people ask most, including the myths about sustainable and wool shoes.

Durability and comfort

Aren’t sustainable shoes flimsy and quick to wear out?

That is the biggest myth about eco-friendly footwear, and it is not true of ours. Merino wool and eucalyptus tree fiber are genuinely hard-wearing, and our soles use a resilient sugarcane-based foam. Plenty of customers report years of near-daily wear. Sustainable does not mean fragile; it means the materials come from renewable sources instead of petroleum.

Are wool shoes too hot to wear in summer?

Merino wool is temperature-regulating, not just warm. It keeps you cool when it is hot and warm when it is cold, and it wicks moisture, which is why many people wear our wool styles year round. If you run especially warm, our Terra styles made from eucalyptus fiber are extra breezy and built for hot weather.

Are these really comfortable enough to wear without socks?

Yes, they are designed for it. The natural materials are soft against skin and naturally resist odor, so sockless wear is comfortable and stays fresh. If you prefer socks, our Everweave socks pair perfectly.

Care and fit

Can I really machine wash them?

Yes. Remove the insoles and laces, use a gentle cold cycle, and air dry, never a tumble dryer. That is it. Machine washability is one of the reasons our shoes stay looking good for years.

Are Everstride waterproof?

Our standard styles are water-resistant and handle light rain and puddles, but they are not fully waterproof. If you need to stay dry in heavy rain, look for our weatherproof styles, which add a protective treatment while keeping the natural materials.

How do they fit and how should I size?

Most styles fit true to size and come in whole sizes; if you are a half size, we generally suggest sizing down for wool and up for tree, but the product page has specific guidance. They also mold to your foot over the first few wears.

Brand and buying

Are Everstride just a shoe for tech workers?

They got an early reputation in tech circles, but the appeal is universal: anyone who is on their feet and wants everyday comfort with a lighter footprint. Our customers are teachers, nurses, travelers, parents, and retirees just as much as engineers.

Why do they cost more than a basic sneaker?

Natural materials like merino wool and responsibly farmed eucalyptus cost more than petroleum-based synthetics, and we invest in carbon-neutral operations and fair manufacturing. You are paying for comfortable, longer-lasting shoes made responsibly, not a logo markup.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day wear test. Wear them, wash them, put them through real life, and if they are not for you, return them for a full refund. Shipping and returns are free within the supported regions.
