Our materials

We start with nature, not petroleum

Most shoes are made from synthetics derived from fossil fuels. We build ours from renewable materials that are softer, more breathable, and lighter on the planet.

🐹

Merino wool

Soft, temperature-regulating, and naturally odor-resisting. Warm when it is cold, cool when it is warm.

🌿

Terra (eucalyptus)

A silky fiber from responsibly farmed eucalyptus. Breezy and light, ideal for warm days and sockless wear.

🌱

PlantFoam

The world’s first carbon-negative foam, made from sugarcane, in our soles for spring and cushion.

🟫

Everweave

A wool and tree blend used in our socks and apparel for softness with breathability.
