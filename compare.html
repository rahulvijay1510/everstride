Why Everstride

Rothy’s, Vessi, and Cariuma all make good sustainable shoes, and Nike makes great athletic ones. Here is where Everstride is genuinely different, and where another brand might fit you better.

vs Rothy’s

Natural fiber, not recycled plastic

Rothy’s makes lovely flats from recycled plastic thread. Everstride starts from renewable natural materials like wool and tree, which many people find softer and more breathable for all-day wear.

vs Vessi

Comfort and carbon over full waterproofing

Vessi’s superpower is being fully waterproof. If you live in the rain, that may win. Everstride prioritizes natural-material comfort and prints a carbon footprint on every pair.

vs Nike

Everyday comfort, not the gym

Nike is unmatched for serious sport. Everstride is built for the other 90% of your steps: commuting, travel, errands, and standing all day, with a much smaller footprint.
